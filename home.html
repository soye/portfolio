VirtualGallery. CS 147. Web app.

Aimed to decrease the awkwardness of new social interactions, VirtualGallery aims to connect you with others in the area based on your interests. Using design thinking, rapid prototyping, and an iterative process, we came up with a web app that provides a fun and effective way of getting to know others.

Does Culture Influence Bicultural Individuals' Ideal Affect?. Tsai Lab. Research poster.

Worked in the Tsai Lab (Culture and Emotion) for nine months, including full-time during the summer. Research focused on the affect of bicultural individuals given cues. Marginally significant results seemed to point to a correlation between cultural cues and changes in affect. The full research poster is here.
